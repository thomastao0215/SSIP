Sino – Swiss Hospital

 

Switzerland is world leading life science hub. Through strategic    partnership with Swiss top research institutes, Sino-Suisse  Innovation Park will invest into the field of oncology, neurology and stem cell.

 

Chinese doctors will be trained by Swiss professors and joint research programs will be set up. All will lead to the creation of a Sino-Swiss Hospital.

 

Top Projects

Sino - Suisse International School

 

There have been a number of private initiatives in Geneva to teach Chinese to  European and Chinese students in Switzerland. Non of them delivers a full Chinese syllabus.

 

In cooperation with Geneva private schools (ex. Institute International de Lancy), Sino-Suisse Innovation Park will invest into a Sino-Suisse International School and finance the arrival of Chinese teachers from top-school in China (ex. Pékin University middle and high school).

Misson Statement

The Sino-Swiss Innovation Park is an association    founded in April 2016 with the support of State of     Geneva and Pekin University.

 

The Sino-Swiss Innovation Park has the objective

to promote innovation in the sector of education, 

environment, TM&T, Fintech and healthcare  

between China and Switzerland.
